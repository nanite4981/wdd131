Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to create a fun and funny experience for people who want to just hop on their device and chill. I'll be doing a mixture of humor and action for the player. Essentially, I'm creating a clicker game website.

Audience

My website is for people who are familiar enough with anime to get the humor around Isekais. It is also for people who enjoy clicker games.

Dynamic elements

Clicker mechanic involving a counter going up and updating a display.

Certain things causing an automatic increase of elements and updating a display.

Upon a click, decreasing certain values to increase others and updating a display.

Potentially saving progress in local storage.

Causing visual elements.
